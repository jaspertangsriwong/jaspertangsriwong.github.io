My name is Jasper Tangsriwong. I am currently a student at the University of California Riverside. I major in Mathematics and plan to go to medical school. My previous job experiences include working at my family owned restaurant called Makoto Sushi , a Thai youth camp called Camp Wat Pa, and at my highschool called Notre Dame High School.

At my family owned sushi restaurant that I worked at, I was a dishwasher and chef. I enjoyed cooking and learning the intricacies of the restaurant world. At the Thai youth camp that I worked at, I was a camp counselor that led workshops and activities that were meant to connect the Thai community together as one.

At my highschool, I was a math tutor that taught anywhere from Algebra to Precalculus. I enjoy teaching math and continuing to learn about it everyday, which is why I decided to become a Mathematics major. I also have my own photography brand that is another part of my job experiences. I consider it a part time job but I still gain a way to capture beautiful moments through multiple perspectives.
